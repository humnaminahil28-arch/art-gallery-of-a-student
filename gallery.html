ART STUDY

01. Hagia Sophia

Pencil sketch of the iconic Hagia Sophia. In this drawing, I emphasized the intricate window patterns and the contrasting shadows of the minarets to capture the grandeur of the monument.

02. GCU lahore

A pencil study exploring the grandeur of Gothic architecture in Lahore. This piece emphasizes the play of light against the tall, slender towers and the sharp, geometric lines of the windows, aiming to capture the academic heritage of the institution

03. Castel Gandolfo

A scenic rendering of the Papal Palace and the surrounding town of Castel Gandolfo. This sketch focuses on the rhythmic placement of Italian windows and the elevation of the town above the lake, utilizing light shading to suggest the Mediterranean sun reflecting off the stone walls.

04. Osaka temple

This sketch explores the contrast between the dark, bold silhouette of a Japanese temple and the intricate, swirling patterns of clouds and a dragon. The background demonstrates a mastery of value and texture, using varied pencil strokes to suggest movement and depth.

05. Disney castle

A detailed architectural study of the iconic fairy-tale castle. This sketch focuses on 'forced perspective' to make the towers appear taller and more grand.

06. Traditional chinese pagoda

This drawing is a balanced, pen-and-ink architectural sketch of a three-tiered East Asian pagoda set within a serene landscape. The structure is built on a hexagonal stone foundation, featuring a central arched portal that serves as the main entrance.
